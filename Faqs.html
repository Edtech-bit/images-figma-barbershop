Do I need to make an appointment at a barbershop?

It depends on the barbershop. Some take walk-ins, while others require appointments. It's always a good idea to check with the shop beforehand.

How much does a haircut cost at a barbershop?

The price can vary depending on location, experience of the barber, and the services you choose. 250 php to 500 php.

Can barbers cut long hair?

Yes, many barbers are trained to cut longer hair, though some specialize in shorter styles. If you have long hair, it's a good idea to confirm with the barber that they are comfortable working with that length.

What's the best way to find a good barber?

You can ask friends or family for recommendations, check online reviews, or visit a barbershop and see if you like the atmosphere and the barber's work.
